What is a Digital Water Level Recorder (DWLR)?

A DWLR is a device used to measure and record water levels at various locations, providing real-time data for monitoring and analysis.

How does the DWLR Monitoring System work?

Our system collects data from DWLRs installed across the country and visualizes it on an interactive map, allowing users to monitor and respond to water level changes effectively.

What are the benefits of using the DWLR Monitoring System?

The system provides real-time monitoring, automated alerts, and data-driven insights, ensuring efficient water management and timely response to any abnormalities.
